Founded in 1898, the Budapest University Athletics Club , BEAC, the University of Budapest, and ELTE have been open to citizens interested in active and healthy lifestyles for more than a hundred years. It would be prove to be of great interest for anyone who loves to take part in physical education which helps maintain a healthy lifestyle. The website also provides information regarding the various sports and tournaments that one might be interested in.

You can apply to sports available from the given list and participate in it according to the schedule. Each and every sport has a specified trainer and venue for it.

You can regiser for course by following the steps below.

1. Log in to http://neptun.elte.hu NEPTUN.

2. Under the "Subjects" tab, select "Register for subject" menu.

3. On the Register for subject page, select the current term and the "Subject type: All subjects" option.

4. Also make sure, that "Subjects currently offered only:" is checked.

5. Write "VTN-" to the "Subject code" field and click on the "List subjects" button.

6. Choose the sport course you want to attend.

7. Click on Register!
