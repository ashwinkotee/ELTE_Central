To find any information related to the immigration office such as the working timing, booking an appointment, find online forms, contact details etc. click on the link below.

http://www.bmbah.hu/jomla/index.php?lang=en

To apply for a fresh residence permit or for an extension of the residence permit, one can sign up to the portal https://enterhungary.gov.hu/eh/?en and then sign in and submit the necessary forms and documents to partially complete the procedure. Once this is done, the processing fee can be paid online or else in person at the immigration office when one goes for providing the biometric data and other necessary procedures if need be.

https://enterhungary.gov.hu/eh/?en
