OTP Bank Group is one of the largest independent financial service providers in Central and Eastern Europe with a full range of banking services for private individuals and corporate clients. OTP Group comprises large subsidiaries, granting services in the field of insurance, real estate, factoring, leasing and asset management, investment and pension funds.

One of the most important parts of proceeding with scholarship is to open a bank account. It is really important to open it in order to get your monthly stipendium from university but in case that you are a self financed student you also need it in order to make your transactions in Hungary.

Note that it is not mandatory to open a bank account at Otp Bank , you can open a bank account at any other bank (it should be a hungarian bank account) but it is recommended to open it at Otp.

Here's some tips on how you can proceed with bank account opening What you will need :

  • Student card or student status
  • Residence permit (it depends from which country you come from sometimes they don-t ask for it)
  • Passport
  • Letter of acceptance
*Note that depending on which country you come from there are some other bureaucratic procedures that you should deal with. You can check the link below about the bank opening procedures.
